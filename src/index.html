About Us

JCE Consulting Engineers was found in 2021 in the heart of the Lowveld in Mpumalanga, Mbombela. The business strives to ensure that people from all walks of life are provided with innovative and sustainable solutions, leading to a better future. We aim to ensure that our professional services and rates offered exceed our Clients expectations. We are a consultant engineering company focusing on the quality of the product delivered, effective management of projects, timeous completion within budget and continuous Client service to meet each and every need.
